How to Buy Physical Gold in UAE • Saudi • Egypt

🇦🇪

UAE (Dubai & Abu Dhabi)

Taxes, best places, storage & mistakes →

Taxes: 0% VAT on 24K bullion • 5% on jewelry

Best places: Dubai Gold Souk, DMCC, Emirates NBD, Malabar Gold

Storage: Bank safe deposit (AED 300–800/year)

Common mistake: Paying high making charges on jewelry

🇸🇦

Saudi Arabia

Taxes, best places, storage & mistakes →

Taxes: 15% VAT on jewelry • 0% on investment bars (SAMA rules)

Best places: Riyadh/Jeddah souks, Al Rajhi Bank, Alinma Bank

Storage: Home safe or bank box

Common mistake: Buying without official Saudi purity stamp

🇪🇬

Egypt

Taxes, best places, storage & mistakes →

Taxes: No VAT on physical gold

Best places: Khan El Khalili, National Bank of Egypt

Storage: Home safe (very common)

Common mistake: Not weighing gold in front of you
