Before you buy

Questions worth asking first

A good gold purchase usually starts with the right question, not the right Instagram reel.

Is 24K always the best investment choice?
Usually for pure metal exposure, yes — but “best” still depends on premium, storage, liquidity, and your actual purpose.
What should I check before buying from a store?
Purity, weight, premium or making charge, invoice clarity, and how resale is handled.
