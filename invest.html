How to Buy Physical Gold in UAE • Saudi • Egypt

🇦🇪 UAE (Dubai & Abu Dhabi)

Taxes: 0% VAT on 24K bullion & coins. 5% on jewelry.

Best places: Dubai Gold Souk, DMCC, banks (Emirates NBD, ADCB), certified dealers like Malabar Gold.

Storage: Bank safe deposit box (AED 300–800/year).

Common mistake: Buying jewelry thinking it’s pure investment (high making charges).

🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia

Taxes: 15% VAT on jewelry. 0% on investment bars/coins (check latest SAMA rules).

Best places: Riyadh & Jeddah gold souks, Al Rajhi Bank, Alinma Bank bullion desks.

Storage: Home safe or bank box.

Common mistake: Ignoring purity stamps (must have Saudi Standards).

🇪🇬 Egypt

Taxes: No VAT on physical gold. Import duties apply if bringing from abroad.

Best places: Khan El Khalili, Cairo gold shops, National Bank of Egypt.

Storage: Home safe (common in Egypt) or bank.

Common mistake: Buying without weighing in front of you.
