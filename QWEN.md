# Gold-Prices

## Product purpose
- This repo is a gold price platform focused on UAE, GCC, and Arab-market users.
- Prioritize trust, clarity, speed, mobile usability, and clean data presentation.
- Do not add fake data, fake shops, fake claims, or placeholder metrics without clearly labeling them.

## Technical rules
- Prefer improving existing files over rewriting large sections unnecessarily.
- Keep the stack lightweight.
- Avoid adding heavy dependencies unless clearly justified.
- Preserve SEO-critical structure, metadata, canonical logic, and page accessibility.
- Keep pages fast and readable on mobile first.

## UX rules
- Prioritize tracker, shops, compare, archive, calculators, and historical pages.
- Keep labels simple and human.
- Avoid clutter, gimmicks, and flashy effects that hurt readability.
- Make important actions obvious.

## Data rules
- Be explicit when data is estimated, derived, delayed, or manually curated.
- Separate verified store data from general market-area content.
- Do not present assumptions as facts.

## Workflow rules
- Before major changes, explain the plan briefly.
- After edits, suggest exact test steps.
- If a command could be risky, ask first.
