Frequently Asked Questions — Gold Price in Islamabad

What is the gold price in Islamabad today?

Live gold prices in Islamabad are updated every 90 seconds from the global spot rate in PKR.

Where to buy gold in Islamabad?

Jinnah Super Market and Aabpara Market have traditional gold shops. Centaurus Mall, Giga Mall, and Blue Area commercial district have modern jewellery retailers.

What gold items are popular in Islamabad?

22K wedding sets and 24K tola bars are most popular. As the capital, Islamabad also has demand for international designer gold brands.

Is gold taxed in Pakistan?

Gold jewellery purchases may be subject to sales tax. Investment gold (bars and coins) has different tax treatment. Consult local regulations for current rates.
