Frequently Asked Questions — Gold Price in Lahore

What is the gold price in Lahore today?

Live gold prices in Lahore are updated every 90 seconds based on the international spot rate in PKR.

Where to buy gold in Lahore?

Anarkali Bazaar and Shah Alami Market are Lahore's traditional gold shopping areas. Gulberg, Liberty Market, and MM Alam Road have modern jewellery stores.

What gold designs are popular in Lahore?

Lahore is famous for Mughal-inspired gold jewellery designs. Traditional bridal sets, polki (uncut diamond) work, and kundan jewellery in 22K gold are specialities.

Are gold prices the same in Lahore and Karachi?

Daily gold rates are set nationally by the Sarafa Association, so base prices are the same. Making charges may vary between cities and shops.

How to verify gold purity in Lahore?

Buy from PSQCA-hallmarked shops. Pakistan has a mandatory hallmarking system. Look for the karat stamp and buy from registered members of the Sarafa Association.
