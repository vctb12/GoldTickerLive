Frequently Asked Questions — Gold Price in Riyadh

What is the gold price in Riyadh today?

The live 24K gold price in Riyadh is updated every 90 seconds on this page based on the global XAU/USD spot rate converted to Saudi Riyal (SAR). The SAR is pegged to the USD at approximately 3.75, so Riyadh gold prices track global spot closely. Check the price cards above for current rates per gram.

Which gold market is most popular in Riyadh?

Al-Zal gold market in central Riyadh and the gold shops in Al-Bataha district are the main trading hubs. Large malls such as Kingdom Centre and Al Nakheel also have prominent jewellery retailers. Traditional souqs typically offer better making-charge rates than mall shops.

Is gold cheaper in Riyadh or Dubai?

The base gold rate (the spot price) is the same in both cities — it's a global benchmark. Differences come from making charges and dealer margins. Dubai's competitive Gold Souk typically has lower making charges than Riyadh mall retailers, but traditional Riyadh souqs are comparable.

What karat is most common for Saudi gold jewellery?

21K and 18K gold are the most common in Saudi Arabia for jewellery, reflecting the preference for harder, more durable alloys in intricate designs. 24K is used for investment bars. 22K is increasingly popular due to Gulf-wide standardisation.

Does Saudi Arabia charge VAT on gold?

Saudi Arabia charges 15% VAT on gold jewellery. Investment-grade gold (coins, bars) meeting specific purity thresholds is VAT-exempt. Always ask the retailer whether the quoted price includes VAT.
