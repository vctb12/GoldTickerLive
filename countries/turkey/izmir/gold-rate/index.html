Frequently Asked Questions — Gold Price in Izmir

What is the gold price in Izmir today?

Where to buy gold in Izmir?

Kemeraltı Bazaar is Izmir's historic gold market district. Konak and Alsancak areas also have jewellery shops. Forum Bornova and Optimum malls have modern retailers.

What gold is popular in Izmir?

Like the rest of Turkey, 22K gold coins and 14K/18K jewellery are popular. Izmir's Aegean influence also means Mediterranean-style gold designs are common.

Is gold cheaper in Izmir than Istanbul?

Base gold prices are identical. Making charges and jewellery designs may vary, with Izmir offering some unique Aegean-style pieces.

Can tourists buy gold in Izmir?

Yes, tourists can buy gold freely. For tax-free shopping, look for Global Blue or Tax Free Easy stickers. Keep receipts for customs at departure.
