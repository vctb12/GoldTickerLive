Frequently Asked Questions — Gold Price in Ankara

What is the gold price in Ankara today?

Live gold prices in Ankara are updated every 90 seconds from the global spot rate converted to TRY.

Where to buy gold in Ankara?

Samanpazarı and Ulus districts have traditional gold jewellers. Modern shopping centres like Ankamall and Kentpark also have reputable gold retailers.

What type of gold is traded in Ankara?

22K Republic Gold Coins and quarter/half gold coins are popular for investment and gifts. 14K and 18K are common for everyday jewellery.

Are gold prices different in Ankara and Istanbul?

Base gold prices are the same across Turkey. Making charges may differ slightly, with Istanbul often having more competitive prices due to higher competition.

How is gold regulated in Turkey?

The Istanbul Gold Exchange (IAB) under Borsa Istanbul sets reference prices. Gold jewellery must carry hallmarks indicating purity and maker's mark.
