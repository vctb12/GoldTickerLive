Frequently Asked Questions — Gold Price in Dubai

What is the gold price in Dubai today?

The live 24K gold price in Dubai is updated every 90 seconds on this page based on the global XAU/USD spot rate and the UAE Dirham fixed peg of 3.6725 to the US Dollar. Check the price cards above for current 24K, 22K, 21K, and 18K rates per gram in AED.

Is the Dubai Gold Souk price the same as the spot price shown here?

The Dubai Gold Souk follows the same spot-derived gold rate, but retail shops add a making charge (أجرة الصنعة) of 3–25 AED per gram for jewellery, depending on design complexity. Our page shows the pure spot equivalent. Investment bars have much smaller premiums (0.5–3%).

Which karat gold is most common in Dubai?

22K is the most popular karat for jewellery in Dubai's Gold Souk. 24K is used for investment bars and coins. 21K is popular for specific designs, and 18K is favoured for detailed white-gold and diamond-set pieces.

How do I convert the gold price to other currencies?

The UAE Dirham is pegged to the US Dollar at 3.6725. To convert: AED price ÷ 3.6725 = USD price. Use our live tracker to view prices in any of 24+ currencies simultaneously.

Where can I buy gold in Dubai?

The Dubai Gold Souk in Deira is the most famous gold market. Other popular areas include Meena Bazaar and Mall of the Emirates. Our Gold Shops directory lists curated locations across Dubai with details on specialties and availability.
