Frequently Asked Questions — Gold Price in Doha

What is the gold price in Doha today?

The live 24K gold price in Doha is updated every 90 seconds based on the global XAU/USD spot rate converted to Qatari Riyal (QAR). The QAR is pegged to the USD at 3.64, meaning Doha gold prices track global spot very closely. Check the price cards above for current rates per gram.

Where is the main gold market in Doha?

Souq Waqif in central Doha is the traditional gold market and a major tourist destination. The Gold Souk inside Souq Waqif offers both traditional Arabian designs and modern jewellery. Villaggio and other major malls also have premium jewellery retailers.

Is gold cheaper in Doha than Dubai?

The base gold spot rate is identical in both cities. Making charges and dealer margins differ — Dubai's Gold Souk is renowned for competitive pricing due to high volume. Doha is competitive but has a smaller trader base. For investment bars, both cities are very similar.

What karat gold is most common in Qatar?

22K gold is the Gulf standard for jewellery, and Qatar follows this convention. 24K is popular for investment bars and gifting. 21K and 18K are available for specific jewellery designs. Qatar Central Bank regulations require hallmarking on all gold sold.

How do I calculate the gold price in QAR?

Use the formula: (XAU/USD spot ÷ 31.1035) × karat purity fraction × 3.64 (QAR/USD peg) = price per gram in QAR. Our page does this automatically and updates every 90 seconds. See our methodology page for full details.
