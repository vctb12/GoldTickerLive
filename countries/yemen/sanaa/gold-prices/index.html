Frequently Asked Questions — Gold Price in Sana'a

What is the gold price in Sana'a today?

Live 24K gold prices in Sana'a are updated every 90 seconds from the global spot rate converted to Yemeni Rial (YER). Currency volatility means YER gold prices can change significantly.

Where to buy gold in Sana'a?

The Old City of Sana'a, a UNESCO World Heritage site, has a traditional gold souk (Souq al-Milh) that has operated for over a thousand years. Modern jewellery shops are found in Hadda and Tahreer districts.

What karat gold is popular in Yemen?

21K gold is the most popular for Yemeni wedding jewellery and traditional silver-gold combinations. Yemeni gold designs are distinctive, featuring geometric patterns and cultural motifs.

How does the Yemeni Rial affect gold prices?

The YER has experienced significant depreciation. Gold in YER has risen sharply, making it an important savings vehicle for Yemenis seeking to preserve purchasing power.

Is gold jewellery culturally important in Sana'a?

Yes, gold and silver jewellery are integral to Yemeni culture. Bridal gold (known as 'mahr') is a central part of wedding traditions, and traditional Yemeni designs are prized throughout the Arabian Peninsula.
