About Gold Weight Units

Gold is measured in many traditional and modern units across different regions. The troy ounce (31.1035 g) is the international standard for precious metals. Tola (11.6638 g) is widely used in South Asia, baht (15.244 g) in Thailand, and mithqal (4.6875 g) in parts of the Middle East. Pennyweight (1.55517 g) is used in the jewellery trade, mainly in North America.
