What making charges cover

They pay for craftsmanship, design complexity, finishing, wastage, and store operating costs.

⚖️

How to compare fairly

Compare making charges separately from the gold value. A lower gold rate with a high making charge may not be cheaper.

❓

Questions to ask

Ask whether charges are per gram or percentage-based, whether VAT is included, and what the resale/buyback policy is.
