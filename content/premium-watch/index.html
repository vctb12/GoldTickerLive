What a premium means

A premium is the extra amount above the bullion-equivalent spot value. It can include making charges, dealer margin, logistics, brand value, and taxes.

🏪

Why shop prices differ

Two shops can quote different final prices for the same karat because design complexity, craftsmanship, stock source, and negotiation policy vary.

  • Ask whether the quote includes VAT.
  • Separate gold value from making charge.
  • Compare against the live 22K or 24K reference baseline.
✅
