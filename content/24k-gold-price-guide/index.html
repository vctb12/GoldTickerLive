What 24K means

24K is near-pure gold, commonly used as the benchmark for spot-linked calculations.

🪙

Bars and coins

Investment-grade bars and coins often trade closer to spot than crafted jewelry, but still include premiums and dealer margins.

💍

Jewelry difference

24K jewelry is softer than 22K or 18K, so durability and design should be considered alongside price.
