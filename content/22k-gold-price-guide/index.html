What 22K means

22K gold is about 91.7% gold, with the rest alloy metals for durability.

💍

Why it is common

It balances high gold content with better durability for jewelry than 24K pure gold.

📈

How price is estimated

The 22K reference price is the 24K spot-linked per-gram value multiplied by 22/24, then converted to local currency.
