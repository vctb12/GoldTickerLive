Start with the AED reference price

Check 24K, 22K, 21K and 18K AED per gram before visiting a shop. This gives you the bullion-equivalent baseline.

🏪

Ask for a breakdown

Request gold value, making charge, VAT, and any premium as separate lines. This makes comparison much easier.

📍

Compare market clusters

Dubai Gold Souk, malls, and neighborhood jewelers can differ in selection, negotiation style, and making charges.
