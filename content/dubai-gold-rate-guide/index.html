Which Dubai rate to check

Most jewelry buyers compare 22K AED per gram, while bars and coins are closer to 24K. Pick the karat that matches the item.

🛒

Dubai Gold Souk context

Souk prices can be negotiable, especially making charges. The gold value should still be anchored to the current spot-linked baseline.

✅

Before paying

Confirm the karat stamp, weight, making charge, VAT treatment, and buyback policy directly with the seller.
