How to read this map

  • Every country starts from the same spot-linked gold value per gram in USD — the color differences come from local VAT and typical jewellery making charges.
  • Darker gold means a higher all-in retail estimate; countries with no FX or market data stay neutral and are listed as unavailable.
  • These are reference estimates for comparison — a shop’s final quote depends on the piece, its making charge and the daily rate board.

Gold value per gram is globally identical in USD — the map colors reflect local VAT and typical making charges only. All figures are spot-linked reference estimates, not final retail quotes or financial advice.
